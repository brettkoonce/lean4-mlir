Once upon a time, there was a little girl named Lily. She loved to play outside in the garden. One day, she saw a butterfly singing with its wings. She wanted to pick it up.
Lily picked it up, and tried to grab the butterfly. Lily flew down the butterfly, but it was too hard. She felt sad.
Suddenly, her butterfly flew away from the butterfly. Lily and the butterfly flew away, trying to fly up. The butterfly flew away and Lily became good friends.
